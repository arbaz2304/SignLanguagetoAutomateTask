GESTURE ALERT SYSTEMS

Patient/User can pass different alert messages making hand gestures in front of camera.

The different types of gestures and associated are messages listed below.

GUIDE FOR LIST OF GESTURES
Doctor Alert

Make a fist by closing all your fingers and thumbs

Nurse Alert

Make a 'victory' gesture using your index and middle finger

Water Alert

Make the gesture of 'all the best'/'water', when you need water

Food Alert

curl your fingers inside and point your thumb downwards

I am fine message

make circle by touching tips of thumb and index finger, keep other fingers open

DRAWING CONTROL SYSTEM

User can draw different geometirc shapes on screen, making hand gestures in front of camera.

The different types of gestures and associated shapes are listed below.

GUIDE FOR LIST OF GESTURES
Draw A cicrle

Make a fist by closing all your fingers and thumbs, draws a circle on screen

Draw a Triangle

stretch your index middle and ring fingers to draw Triangle

Turn the cursor to left

To turn the drawing cursor stretch your index finger

Turn the cursor to right

To turn the drawing cursor stretch your index finger and middle finger

Draw Rectangle

stretch your index finger and thumb outside to draw Rectangle!
